In mathematics, an [[[[file:202101181829-expressions.org][expression]]]] or mathematical expression is a finite
combination of [[[[file:202101181833-symbol.org][symbol]]]] that is [[[[file:202101181834-well_formed.org][well-formed]]]] according to rules that depend
on the context.  Mathematical symbols can designate numbers ([[[[file:202101181834-constants.org][constants]]]]),
[[[[file:202101181834-variables.org][variables]]]], [[[[file:../mathematics/202101181836-operations.org][operations]]]], [[[[file:../mathematics/202101181835-functions.org][functions]]]], brackets, punctuation, and
grouping to help determine [[[[file:../mathematics/202101181836-order_of_operations.org][order of operations]]]], and other aspects of
[[[[file:../logic/202101181836-logical_syntax.org][logical syntax]]]].
